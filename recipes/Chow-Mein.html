Chow-Mein

Description

Nepali Chow Mein is an mix of nepali and tibietan fusion that creates a savory and filling dish while being very easy and quick to make.

Ingridents

  • Rice Noodle or Spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp. of vegitable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chow Mein masala
  • Carrots
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Cabbage
  • Chilli
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil the noodle of your choice following the direction on the package, add some oil to the water to make the noodle not stick to the pot.
  2. Once the noodle is boiled, pour it in a strainer to drain the water and make sure to wash the noodle gently with cold water so it maintains firm structure.
  3. Heat up a pan, once its heated add 2 tbsp. of vegitable oil.
  4. Once the oil is heated, add the chopped oil and stir it constantly until its golden brown.
  5. Add the chicken in the pan and stir it till its golden brown.
  6. Add the cabbage, chilli, and cabbage next along with some salt and chow mein masala.
  7. Continue to stir the contents of the pan for 5 more minutes, making sure nothing is burnt.
  8. Add the noodles next and stir well.
  9. Thats all, enjoy your chow mein!
