Momos

Description

Nepali dumplings, widely known as "Momos" are among the most popular and delicious among the nepali dishes. Momos are relatively easy to make but does require some time and preparation. Momos can be made with variety of ingridents such as beef, pork, chickens but today we will be making vegitable momos with cabbage.

Ingridents

  • Momo Wrappers
  • 4 finely dicied Onions
  • 1 whole finely diced cabbage
  • 2 tbsp of finely diced ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • Momo Masala
  • MSG
  • Vegitable Oil
  • Spray Oil

Steps

  1. Get a large bowl to mix all of your ingridents and out all the cabbage, onions, and ginger in it.
  2. In your mixture, add 1 tsp. of MSG, 2 tbsp. of Momo Masala, and some salt for taste.
  3. Heat up 5 tbsp. of vegitable oil and pour it in your bowl of mixture over the other ingridents.
  4. Mix your ingridents in the bowl throughly.
  5. Get your momo wrapper and in wrapper put about 1 and 1/2 tbsp of the mixture in bowl.
    6. Fold the wrapper by pinching the top to make it look like a pouch or dumplings.
  6. Once the mixtre or your wrapper is finished, place all the dumpling in an dumpling steamer and use spray oil on the steamer so dumpling doesn't stick to it.
  7. Fill the large steamer pot with water and put the dumpling steamer on top if it to cook the dumpling through steam.
  8. Steam the momos for 15 minutes and enjoy!
